About Appcode Academy

The AppCode Academy is an organization that seeks to teach and train people who wish to start a career in Information Technology.

Everything about us is told through our products and students. Our slogan is "Carry What Matters”, which means that we’re designing products and courses with functionality that encourages carrying only what you need. The Appcode Academy was birth about 20years ago and we've worked hard throughout the previous years to reach where we are today. From (2) students to over 500 alumnias and still counting. We want to boast that same thought in the way that our students or customers approach life. That is: leaving the drama behind, focusing on the positive, and carrying only the essential things with us in life. And that is a direct reflection of what we have been proving since the birth of this institution.

20 years ago we saw a problem, Found a solution and ever since then we've been working hand -in-hand with industry experts in providing a form of education that builds up students for the job market

AppCode Academy is now recognized as one of the finest Tech training and recruitment community in Africa, with a proven track record in building students that are ready for the job market , with critical thinking abilities and an entrepreneurial mindset, and the ability to solve complex problems. Our Mission

The mission of Appcode is to educate a new generation of ethical leaders in Africa; to cultivate within our students the critical thinking skills, the concern for others and the courage it will take to transform a continent. we aim to advance knowledge and educate students in the field of IT, and other areas of scholarship that will best serve the nation and the world in the 21st century and beyond.

Our Vision

To make the world our campus through industry partnerships and experiential learning by teaching the students of today the skills of tomorrow forever.

Shared Values

Forward-thinking: We strive to be the leaders of change. We anticipate hurdles, innovate, and make incremental improvements by embracing an innovative and proactive attitude.

We adopt an equity framework that proactively seeks to make Appcode accessible, affordable, and inclusive for all people.
